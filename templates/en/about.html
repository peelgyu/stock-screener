About StockInto

Stock analysis powered by the criteria of 5 legendary investors — free, instant, transparent.

🎯 What we do

StockInto helps individual investors quickly vet a stock against time-tested investment philosophies before making a decision. "Would Warren Buffett buy this?", "Does it pass Graham's margin of safety test?" — we answer in under one second.

We collect 30+ financial metrics (PER, PBR, ROE, debt-to-equity, revenue growth, etc.) for 5,000+ listed companies in the US and Korea, then score each stock against the criteria of five legendary investors. Results are presented as A through F grades, plus the underlying data so you can verify our reasoning.

👥 The 5 Investors

Warren Buffett
Quality + Value
"Buy a wonderful company at a fair price." High ROE, low debt, durable moats, consistent earnings.
Benjamin Graham
Defensive Value
PER ≤ 15, PBR ≤ 1.5, current ratio ≥ 200%, dividend, margin of safety.
Peter Lynch
GARP (Growth at a Reasonable Price)
PEG < 1, EPS growth > 15%, low institutional ownership (still under-the-radar).
William O'Neil
CAN SLIM Momentum
Quarterly EPS +25%, near 52-week high, accelerating volume, market direction.
Philip Fisher
Quality Growth
High operating/gross margins, sustained R&D, multi-year EPS CAGR ≥ 10%.

📊 Key Features

5-investor scoring

21 criteria across the 5 frameworks, scored automatically with sector-relative thresholds.

Fair value (4 methods)

DCF, PER multiple, Graham number, and analyst consensus — weighted by sector.

Multi-year trend

5-year ROE consistency, gross margin stability, revenue/EPS CAGR.

Fear & Greed Index

6-indicator weighted gauge: RSI, 52-week range, moving averages, volatility.

Daily market briefing

Auto-generated overnight: US/KR indices, FX, top movers, sector news.

Korean stocks support

Hard-to-find: KOSPI/KOSDAQ analysis using DART (Korean SEC equivalent) financial data.

🔍 Data Sources (transparency)

  • US stocks — Yahoo Finance (prices, 15-20 min delayed) + SEC EDGAR (financials, TTM 10-K/10-Q self-calculated)
  • Korean stocks — FinanceDataReader (KRX prices) + DART OpenAPI (financial statements). yfinance is intentionally not used for Korean stocks due to data quality issues.
  • News — Naver Search API (Korean) for relevant market signals
  • Sector benchmarks — 11 sectors with custom thresholds (technology, financial services, healthcare, energy, etc.)
Why this matters: Most free tools rely on yfinance, which is known to have errors and gaps for non-US markets. StockInto uses each market's authoritative regulatory source — SEC for US, DART for Korea — to give you the most accurate fundamental data possible.

👤 Operator

StockInto is a personal project built and operated by an individual investor with 7 years of personal investing experience.

✍ Why I built this

When my own conviction wasn't strong enough, I'd turn to Warren Buffett's quantitative criteria and run the analysis myself. But every time, pulling DART filings, calculating PER and ROE, organizing five-year trends, and comparing them to sector averages — all of it was tedious.

I also realized that my own style doesn't always align with Buffett's principles, and that evaluating a stock through one master's lens alone wasn't enough. Looking at the same stock through Graham, Lynch, O'Neil, and Fisher's frameworks revealed different angles, and this multi-perspective comparison genuinely helped my judgment.

From these two motivations — to reduce the tedium and see all five perspectives at once — I built and published a tool that translates the quantitative criteria of five legendary investors into code.

🛡 Operating Principles

  • Aside from advertising, no individual stock is ever recommended or promoted.
  • All analyses are verifiable against public data (SEC EDGAR · DART).
  • Core analysis is accessible immediately, with no extra steps required.
  • Corrections to filings are auto-refreshed within 24 hours.
