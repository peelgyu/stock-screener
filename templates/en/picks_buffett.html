🎯 Buffett-Style Stocks — 20 Picks

📅 2026-05-06 · 12 US + 8 Korean · StockInto Curation

"Buy a wonderful company at a fair price" — Warren Buffett

Buffett's investment philosophy compresses to 5 axes — consistent ROE 15%+, low debt, strong moat, stable margins, positive FCF. This page lists candidate stocks aligned with that philosophy. Click any card to see StockInto's automatic scoring against the 9 quantitative criteria.

🇺🇸 US — Stocks Buffett Owns or Resembles

🇺🇸BRK-B
Berkshire Hathaway
Buffett's own holding company. Diversified across insurance, railroads, energy, consumer goods. The original.
🇺🇸AAPL
Apple
Berkshire's largest single holding (as of 2025). Strong brand moat + buybacks + consistent ROE.
🇺🇸KO
Coca-Cola
Buffett holding since 1988. Global brand moat + stable margins + 60+ years of dividend growth.
🇺🇸MSFT
Microsoft
Enterprise SW moat + Office/Azure lock-in + consistent 35%+ ROE multi-year.
🇺🇸JNJ
Johnson & Johnson
Pharma + medical devices + consumer goods diversification. AAA credit rating (same as US government).
🇺🇸PG
Procter & Gamble
Brand portfolio of consumer staples (Gillette, Pampers, Tide). 60+ year dividend aristocrat.
🇺🇸V
Visa
Payment network effect = strong moat. Operating margin 60%+. Berkshire holding.
🇺🇸MA
Mastercard
Duopoly with Visa. Same network effect moat. 50%+ margins.
🇺🇸COST
Costco
Membership lock-in + economies of scale = pricing power. Munger's favorite.
🇺🇸WMT
Walmart
Scale moat. 50+ years of dividend growth. Stable FCF.
🇺🇸MCD
McDonald's
Global brand + real estate assets. Recession-resistant via franchise model.
🇺🇸HD
Home Depot
#1 in US home improvement. High ROE + aggressive buybacks.
